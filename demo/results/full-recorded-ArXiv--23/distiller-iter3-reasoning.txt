An elegant and robust way to solve this is to avoid the fragile date-range filter entirely. When searching for very recent papers (like "yesterday"), a strict date filter often returns 0 results (especially on weekends or due to timezone/update delays), which confuses agents into thinking their search failed. 

Instead, searching with a broader date range and sorting the results by submission date allows you to visually inspect and count the papers from the target date.
